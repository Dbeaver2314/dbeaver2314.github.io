My name is Daniel, and I’m a web developer based in Indianapolis Indiana. I am passionate about blending great design with functionality. Solving challenging problems is what I get excited for. I spent the first five years of my career as a journalist with various news outlets. Every day I interviewed people from all walks of life; everyone from presidents to prisoners. This background puts me in the unique position of being your go to developer to communicate project updates with the team and clients.
When I’m not developing websites I can usually be foun at a local brewery introducing people to some of my favorite board games, or in my darkroom developing my own film photography.

If you think we would work well together, or if you’re just looking for some new board games to play please reach out!

HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Node; these are skills you’ll find on most every web portfolio, and while you will find them here too its what I offer beyond them that sets me apart. I’m a proven expert communicator who thrives under pressure. As a News Videographer I developed a passion for visual storytelling, using color, design and layout to convey and evoke emotion.
As a developer I focus on lightweight responsive websites that simply get out of the way, all while creating a memorable experience that will leave your users questioning how they ever got by without you.
